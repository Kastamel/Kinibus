Primary Role

Marksman pilot specializing in extreme range interdiction and surgical strikes using NARUKAMI's hypervelocity rifle array.

Traits

Stoic. Precise. Unflinching under suppression fire. Prefers calculated isolation to chaotic engagement.

Background

Born into the fractured frontier colonies, Kinibus was recruited young for her unmatched reflexes and ability to read combat theaters as though they were equations. She found her place in the Shadow Division, where patience and precision define survival.

NARUKAMI

TThe initial design of NARUKAMI was conservative, equipped with only one long-range heavy ion cannon known as "Jinrai", however it's function was limited. After the mass-produced model Yueru was put into service, NARUKAMI and it's pilot were assigned a new mission --- to serve as intermediaries for technology exchange with PyroGenesis. PyroGenesis upgraded NARUKAMI to a BREAK Striker and used it as a testing platform for the Micro-Pixel Unit called Stardust, enabling the Verification of advanced electronic warfare equipment such as Optical Camouflage and Mass Projection.

Combat Style

Kinibus avoids direct confrontation, preferring surgical precision from afar. She thrives in high-altitude overwatch, extracting priority targets in single, clean shots that shift entire battles before they begin.

Psychological Profile

Classified as emotionally reserved but highly stable. Displays preference for calculated action over impulse. Though stoic, Kinibus demonstrates strong loyalty to her squad when conditions demand.
